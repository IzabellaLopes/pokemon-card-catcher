How to Play


Welcome to Pokémon Card Catcher!


This is a memory card game that will put your memory skills to the test.
Your objective is to find the matching pairs of Pókemon cards as quickly as possible.
With different levels of difficulty to choose from, you can challenge yourself and have fun while becoming a master card catcher!
